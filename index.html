I’m Sutharsan,

A passionate amateur photographer with an eye for the extraordinary beauty of nature. Through my lens, I strive to capture moments that tell stories and evoke emotions.

"Photography is an art of observation. It’s about finding something interesting in an ordinary place… I’ve found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them." — Elliott Erwitt

My Journey

My journey into photography began with a simple curiosity to preserve everyday beauty. Over time, this evolved into a passion for capturing landscapes, portraits, and the interplay of light and shadow. Photography is my creative outlet and connection to the world.

Captured Chronicles

Captured Chronicles is a visual diary of my explorations. Here, you'll find photographs reflecting my perspective and vision, from misty mountains to serene landscapes. Each image is an invitation to see the world through my eyes.

"The best thing about a photograph is that it never changes, even when the people in it do." — Andy Warhol

Disclaimer


This website, Captured Chronicles, is designed by Sutharsan solely for educational purposes. The HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills applied here are part of a practical application to showcase my abilities in web development. Instead of creating clone websites, I wanted to build something meaningful and useful, so I created Captured Chronicles as my first web development project.

I capture photographs during my free time and while traveling, focusing on everyday memories. This site serves as a platform to share my passion for photography and improve my skills, allowing me to share my work with viewers through a website I built myself.

My Gadget


The gadget I use for my photography is the VIVO 1904 Vivo Y12. It features a 13 MP primary lens, an 8 MP ultra-wide lens, and a 2 MP depth sensor, offering a versatile camera setup perfect for capturing detailed shots, wide landscapes, and beautiful portraits.
